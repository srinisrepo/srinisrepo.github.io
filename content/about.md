I'm Sriniketh, an undergrad building things — mostly simulation, control, and autonomous systems. This blog is where the work that usually stays in a private repo gets written up properly.

I'm not writing from the other side of expertise. I'm writing while I'm still figuring things out, which means the derivations are the ones I actually worked through myself, and the mistakes are ones I actually made. If something here is wrong, it's wrong because I didn't know better yet, not because I was hiding the hard part.

Posts get updated when I learn the earlier version was incomplete or wrong. There's no polish pass to make old work look better than it was — just a running record of what I built and what it took to understand it.
